Origins

The computer that we know today started out as machines that look nothing like what we all use today, these computers were called mainframes, large, roomsized computers with spinning reel to reel tapes and flashing lights.

Updates & Upgrades

As computers became more power, their user interfaces became easier to use, this then made computers accesible to a larger amount of people, such as people who aren't "tech savvy."
